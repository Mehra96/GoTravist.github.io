A Few Words About Us

Gotravist as a firm was incorporated on 1 st January, 2017 and since then have been up and running.
We have felicitated and introduced treks in Uttarakhand to almost more than 500+ new travelers and trekkers which make Gotravist very sporty and adventurous.
With trekking, camping and hiking in hand we also provide Char Dham yatra, home stays, hostel stay (newest addition), transportation, river rafting, campsites and hotel stays.

The idea behind GOTRAVIST is just a mixture and mashup of tourists, trekker and travelers coming together and rejuvenating in natures lap.

With trekking, camping and hiking in hand we also provide Char Dham yatra, home stays, hostel stay (newest addition), transportation, river rafting, campsites and hotel stays.

AboutWhat makes us different?

We don’t make comparisons on the grounds of quantity of trekkers or travelers with any company but we definitely make every individual feel different because we are:

Pocket friendly

We believe in making your travel and trek pocket friendly. If you are unable to visit any place or trek any peak then we are there for your assistance. We try and accommodate pocket friendly members to our group because we understand that nature can never be compared or calculated in monetary terms!

Eco friendly

Till date, we have never ever littered even a single non-biodegradable wastage in the Himalayas and this practice makes us much more eco centric and ecofriendly. We don’t involve in fixing PERMANENT CAMPS in any of our treks because that is devastating for the wildlife & natural beauty of that particular region.

Go-Solo

If you are a solo backpacker then we will help you achieve what you seek as a solo gotravister! Be it a trek, a tourist destination or a travel place we are here to make your solo trip much more creative and learning based one.

Employment

We aim in fighting for the most vulnerable plight of our state Uttarakhand on the topic of migration and thus by providing work to only local people of hills and villages living in difficult conditions.

Social Awareness

< This is going to be our latest CSR activity which will help in reaching out to uneducated and villagers about practices like sanitization, precautionary measures, hand wash, using washroom and various social awareness programs.

Customization

We aim in building customized groups with customized packages.
