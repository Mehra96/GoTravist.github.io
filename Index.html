Brahmatal Trek

A peak winter trek, with delicious views of Mt.Trishul and Mt.Nanda Ghunti.
Brahmatal is a lake nestling in the depths of Uttarakhand Himalayas. Winter sweeps alluringly on the slopes here, making Brahmatal one of the rare Himalayan destinations available for trekking in December, January, February, and March.

Deoria Tal-Chopta–Tunganath and Chandrashila trek

The whole tour covers a swoop to the Chandrashila Peak, passing by the enchanting Deoriatal lake side, and opens up a gallery of stunning snow peaks all the way.

The Kanatal Orchids

Kanatal, a quaint little hamlet in Uttarakhand state of India, is perched at an altitude of over 8,500 feet. Kanatal offers spectacularly breath taking views of the snow-clad Himalayan mountain range surrounded by lush green forests.
